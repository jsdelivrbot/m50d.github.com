## The Difference Between Precision and Accuracy

[A year ago](https://www.chrisstucchio.com/blog/2013/basic_income_vs_basic_job.html), Chris Stucchio (AKA yummyfajitas) wrote on the subject of basic income. He wrote some code, ran some monte carlo simulations, and came out with some quite shocking results; a basic job was far, far superior to a basic income.
